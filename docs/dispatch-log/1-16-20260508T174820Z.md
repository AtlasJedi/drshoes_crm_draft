## Concerns
- `drawerOrder` fetch errors are swallowed into `fetchError=true` ‚Äî this means a 404 for a bad `orderId` also hides the list. Low risk for M1 (orderId comes from valid row clicks), but a more granular error boundary would be cleaner in M2.
- `OrderDrawerCoreFields` uses `defaultValue` (uncontrolled) on all fields so local edits aren't lost on re-render; however, if the parent re-fetches and passes a new `order` prop the fields won't reset. Acceptable for M1; track as future improvement.
